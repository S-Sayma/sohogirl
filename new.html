About Soho Girl

  • store_mall_directoryWho We Are & What We Do

    Fashion is a form of art and you are a beautiful canvas. What you wear represents who you are. Soho Girl believes in Fahion For All. Our mission is to bring trending runway designs to the streets, lighting up cities and towns across America with affordable couture fashion and innovative styles.

    Headquartered in Downtown Los Angeles, Soho Girl’s location allows us to work exclusively with the talented designers of the infamous LA Fashion District . Growing from a Soho NYC brick & mortar to an international online fashion destination, the Soho Girl team has been continuously scouting the globe for cutting edge fashion since 1999.

  • tag_faces100% Happy Experience

    Your most beautiful accessory is your smile. At Soho Girl, we strive to make your shopping experience fun, stress-free, and something you feel good about. Most of our products are designed and ethically manufactured right here in America . If we do source from an international fabricator, we will let you know in the product description.

  • Insured Orders & Same Day Shipping

    Keep calm and shop on. As a Google Trusted Store, every Soho Girl order is insured up to $1,000. If you get your order in before 4pm (Pacific), we ship it to you that same day. Late night shopper? We’ll get your goods postmarked and on their way the next business day.

    We believe the fashionistas of world deserve a little love which is why we offer free USPS shipping on all orders over $75. Orders under $75 are shipped USPS for just $6.95. Standard shipping takes about 2-5 business days. Need your goods in a hurry? We got you. Just select USPS Next Day Air, 2nd Day Air, or 3rd Day Air when checking out. USPS delivers Monday through Friday, save for holidays. Weekend delivery is available in some areas and, as much as we would love to, we can’t guarantee it.

    Please double check that your shipping address is accurate and secure. Soho Girl will safely package and send your order, but once it is marked delivered by the USPS, we are released of all liability.

    Globetrotting Fashionistas, we’ll cover international shipping for all orders over $99. International Orders under $99 ship for just $19.99. International orders are shipped the same day they are placed (see above) and take anywhere from 3-7 days to arrive, depending on your location. For a list of countries we are able ship internationally, scroll to the bottom of this page.

  • markunread_mailboxStress-free Return Policy

    Life is too good to stress over returns. We want you to love your purchase as much as we do. If you get your order and aren’t completely satisfied with your items, simply package them back up and include a note with your order number and either the word "REFUND" or "EXCHANGE"

    If you choose to EXCHANGE, be sure to include the new size you want and a contact email or phone number in case new shipping costs are incurred.

    Ship returns to: Soho Girl, 1151 Santee. St. Los Angeles, CA 90015

    Returns must be postmarked back to us within 14 days of delivery. All return items must be in brand new condition, no signs of wear and tags still attached. Returns from orders that received free shipping will be refunded less the $6.95 shipping cost. All sequin items are subject to a 25% restocking fee.

    The exception to our awesome return policy is swimsuits and Halloween costumes, for obvious hygienic reasons, and Shail K and Elizabeth K dresses, for contractual reasons.

    Soho Girl’s goal is to make you happy and beautiful. We are pleased to offer easy returns and ask that you hold onto your return tracking and shipping information until your return is fully processed.

  • Cookies

    Cookies are commonly found on most websites. A cookie is a small file which asks permission to be placed on your computer's hard drive. Once you agree, the file is added and the cookie helps analyze web traffic or lets you know when you visit a particular site. Cookies allow web applications to respond to you as an individual. The web application can tailor its operations to your needs, likes and dislikes by gathering and remembering information about your preferences.

    We use traffic log cookies to identify which pages are being used. This helps us analyze data about web page traffic and improve our website in order to tailor it to customer needs. We only use this information for statistical analysis purposes and then the data is removed from the system.

    Overall, cookies help us provide you with a better website, by enabling us to monitor which pages you find useful and which you do not. A cookie in no way gives us access to your computer or any information about you, other than the data you choose to share with us. You can choose to accept or decline cookies. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser setting to decline cookies if you prefer. This may prevent you from taking full advantage of the website.
